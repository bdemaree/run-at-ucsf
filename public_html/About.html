About Us

Run at UCSF  is a group of Parnassus and Mission Bay UCSFer's who like to run (or do it out of desperation). The origins of this group are with the 2018 JP Morgan Challenge Team, and a bunch of lost-looking MS1's who are learning how to cope. Our mission as a campus organization is to bring the UCSF community together through running, and in doing so provide an athletic and social outlet for all.
